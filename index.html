AstroAI-Lab: Interdisciplinary research group on Astrophysics and Machine Learning.

AstroAI-Lab is a research group led by Dr. Tobias Buck that combines computational astrophysics and machine learning to study the formation and evolution of galaxies. AstroAI-Lab is jointly located at the interdisciplinary center for scientific computing (IWR) and the institute for theoretical astrophysics of the university Heidelberg (ITA). Our research is funded by the Carl-Zeiss-foundation within their Nexus program.

Research Idea

Develop innovative methods for scientific computing by synthesising physical laws and machine learning.
